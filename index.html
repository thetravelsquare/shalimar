  • LEELA (The Scent of Rain)

    The Leela Parfum is an enthralling and voluptuous fragrance, eloquent, symbolizing the woman who is strong and sure of her charm, sensual and feminine, always glamorous. Natural and resonant ingredients create a captivating olfactory story. Woody and warm notes vibrate through the heart and base. Deep Sandalwood, heady Tahitian vanilla and unique grey amber accords strike a complementary contrast with the inherent femininity found in exotic floral notes of soft Casablanca Lilly, feminine orange blossom, and delicate Ylang Ylang.

    At Shalimar we believe in our creations, we have curated this fragrance for the modern woman who's strong yet feminine, sophisticated yet charismatic. She embodies the very essence of chic elegance and style.

  • RUH GULAB (Rose Attar)

    Sexy, fresh Oriental fragrance recalls the irrepressible spirit of the young Girl in Town. The Mademoiselle Perfume by Shalimar, is a unique fragrance that was launched by Shalimar in 2018. This citrusy rosy fragrance edition is the lighter and sweeter version of the rose fragrance. This fragrance has top notes of Gulab, Rose Petals, Ittar, and Fresh grapefruit. Its middle notes feature jasmine, rose, and litchi. The base notes consist of patchouli, Bourbon vanilla, vetiver, and white musk. This fragrance is long lasting and has moderate sillage.

    The Shalimar Presents a modern bright and dazzling freshness, embellishing the rose blossom and orris which were the core of La Vie Est Belle L’Eclat.

  • AMBER (Refreshing Frangipani Attar)

    Amber by Shalimar is a Aromatic Aquatic fragrance for men. It was launched in 2020. The fresh bergamot and marine accords are paired with the deep, captivating radiance of incense – an inspiring and seductive rich material, creating a profoundly sensual aromatic men's fragrance that holds a special fascination for Amber.

    Top notes are Lime, Lemon, Bergamot, Jasmine, Orange, Mandarin Orange and Neroli; middle notes are Sea Notes, Jasmine, Calone, Peach, Freesia, Hiacynth, Cyclamen, Rosemary, Violet, Coriander, Nutmeg, Rose and Mignonette; base notes are White Musk, Cedar, Oakmoss, Patchouli and Amber.
